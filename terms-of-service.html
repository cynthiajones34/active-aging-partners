Services Provided

Active Aging Partners offers in-person companion wellness services to seniors in our service area, including:

  • Walking & Wellness: Structured companionship walks in local neighborhoods, parks, and trails at a pace suited to the client
  • Companionship & Engagement: Meaningful in-home visits including conversation, games, reading, technology assistance, meal companionship, and plant and pet care
  • Errand Support: Running errands on the senior's behalf — grocery shopping, pharmacy pickups, and other local errands — without transporting the senior client

All services are provided within our designated service area covering Tucker, North Decatur, North Druid Hills, Chamblee, Brookhaven, and Doraville, Georgia. Services outside this area may be available at our discretion.

Important: Active Aging Partners provides companion wellness services only. We are not a medical provider, home health agency, or licensed caregiver service. Our staff do not provide medical advice, administer medications, perform clinical functions, or transport senior clients.

Scope of Services

Our staff are trained companions, not licensed healthcare professionals. The following are explicitly outside the scope of our services:

  • Transporting senior clients in a vehicle at any time
  • Administering, managing, or advising on medications of any kind

If a senior client's needs exceed the scope of our services, we will notify the family contact and may refer to appropriate licensed care providers.

Common Questions

Before you reach out, check these

How much does a session cost?
All services are $50/hour. Transport and errand sessions include a $0.67/mile gas surcharge for miles driven on your behalf, itemized on every invoice.
How do I book a session?
Once you create a free account, you can book directly from your client portal. Pick your service, choose a date and time, and you're all set.
What happens after each visit?
You'll receive a detailed session report by email — including mood, activities, miles walked, and any notes. Family members can also access reports in the portal anytime.
Do I need to commit to a schedule?
Not at all. You can book one session at a time, or set up a recurring schedule — whatever works best for your family.
How do payments work?
Invoices are sent automatically after each session via Stripe. You can pay securely by card directly from your email or client portal.
Are you background checked?
Yes. All Active Aging Partners staff are fully background checked. Your loved one's safety and your peace of mind are our first priority.

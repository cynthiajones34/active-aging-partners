What We Offer

Services designed for independence and joy

🚶
Walking & Wellness

Regular walking sessions to keep seniors active, energized, and socially engaged. Parks, neighborhoods, trails — wherever feels right.

$50 / hour
🏥
Medical Appointments

Safe, reliable transport and companionship to doctor visits, specialist appointments, physical therapy, and more. We take notes and keep families informed.

$50 / hour + mileage
🛒
Errands & Outings

Grocery runs, pharmacy pickups, hair and nail appointments, and any other errands that keep life running smoothly and independently.

$50 / hour + mileage
Gas Surcharge Policy
For transport services, a mileage surcharge of $0.67/mile (current IRS rate) is added for miles driven on your behalf. Calculated from client's home and back. Always itemized on your invoice.
Simple Process

Getting started is easy

1
Sign Up

2
